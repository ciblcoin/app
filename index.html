Powering Sustainable Agriculture with Blockchain

CIBL is a community-first Solana token designed to fund and scale organic farming, reduce greenhouse impacts, and connect producers directly with conscious consumers. Using transparent tokenomics, on-chain grants, and an engaged DAO, we drive measurable environmental outcomes.

How Cibl ($CIB) Helps?

Cibl ($CIB) funds pilot projects, certifies practices on-chain, and pays farmers for verified impact (e.g. increased soil organic carbon). The token can be used as payment in an ethical marketplace, and DAO votes ensure funds are used where they deliver measurable impact.
